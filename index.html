Structure of your answer (Template đối chiếu):

Introduction • Introduce the topic: "People have differing views with regard to + topic (noun phrase)...."
• Give your answer: "While some people hold the opinion that + view 1 (clause), I believe that + view 2 (clause)"
Body 1 • Introduce view 1 and give explanation(s) and example(s) to support this view:
"On the one hand, there are some reasons why a common belief is that + view 1 (clause).... The main cause is + supporting idea 1 (clause).... Another reason is + supporting idea 2 (clause) ..."
Body 2 • State that you support view 2 and give explanation(s) and example(s) to support the second view:
"On the other hand, I believe that + view 2 (clause). Firstly, ..... Secondly, ..... Finally, ......"
Conclusion "In conclusion, while + view 1 (clause), I believe that + view 2 (clause)"
